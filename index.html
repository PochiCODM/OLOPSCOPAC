UNIT LIBRARIES

College Learning Resource Center

The College Learning Resource Center is located at the Ground Floor Lobby, Left Wing of the NewGen Building.  The purpose of the library is to serve the needs of the faculties and students of the College Department.

The LRC provides collection of books, periodicals and theses that answer to the teaching, learning and research needs of the College based on the curriculum.

Access of materials in the College Learning Resource Center is thru Koha Integrated Library System and has adopted the Library of Congress Classification for the organization of books for easy retrieval.

Special Policies:

Only students and faculty members of the College are allowed to borrow books from the library.  However, faculty members and students from other units may also avail of the library materials thru Inter Library Loan (request should be made from the Unit Library).

Likewise, only faculty members and students of the College Department are allowed to use the Internet stations for a maximum of one hour, or longer if there is no other researchers.

*Opens Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM; Saturdays, 8:00 AM to 12:00 NN.

High School Learning Resource Center

The Learning Resource Center (LRC) of the High School is primarily located on the 2 nd floor of the St. Benedict Building. It contains a substantial collection of learning resources, print, non- print and online collection of resources that supports the curriculum organized into various sections and subject contents. This library generally serves the needs of the students under secondary curriculum, as well as faculty members and student researchers who are in need of relevant reference materials for research, study and leisure activities.

*Opens from Monday to Friday, 6:30 AM to 8:00 PM; Saturdays, 8:00 AM to 12:00 NN.

Grade School Learning Resource Center

The Learning Resource Center (LRC) primarily serves the needs of the OLOPSC Grade School community from Kinder 1-6. It is located at the 2 nd Floor of St. Elizabeth and St. Gabriel Buildings.

As an essential component and support to the instructional program of the school, the LRC provides a reasonable amount of resource materials both print, non-print and online resources arranged in various subject contents following the Dewey Decimal Classification and access thru Koha Integrated Library System for easy access and retrieval.

*Opens from Monday to Friday, 6:30 AM to 5:00 PM; Saturdays, 8:00 AM to 12:00 NN.

Pre-School Learning Resource Center

The Pre-School Learning Resource Center provides young learners with a welcoming space for reading, exploration, and discovery. The collection comprises of picture books, board books, classic and award-winning titles, books about community helpers, social-emotional learning, science and nature. Additionally, the library provides manipulative resources that allow children to engage in exploratory play, learn through experimentation, and develop problem-solving skills.

Spaces for play and physical exercise are also provided wherein sportsmanship among children is being develop.

*Opens from Monday to Friday, 7:00 AM to 3:00 PM; Saturdays, 8:00 AM to 12:00 NN.
